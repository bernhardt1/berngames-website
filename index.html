Hero King

Fast-paced mobile roguelike with tower defense roots.

Hero King is a fast-paced mobile roguelike with tower defense roots, built for short, satisfying play sessions and endless progression.

Inspired by games like The Tower, survivor-style roguelikes, and classic tower defense, Hero King blends active combat, strategic upgrades, and repeatable runs into a loop that feels powerful, readable, and addictive on mobile.

The loop

Each run is intentionally short and punchy. You jump into battle, fight escalating waves of enemies, earn upgrades, grow stronger, and eventually fall, only to return stronger on the next run.

Hero King is designed from the ground up for iOS and Android, with one-handed play, clear UI, and performance-friendly visuals that feel premium without being noisy or overwhelming.

Playstyle

Combat is automatic, but positioning, upgrades, relics, and timing decisions matter. Every choice should feel readable and powerful on a phone screen.

Core gameplay

Tower defense strategy meets roguelike progression and tight mobile pacing.

Tower defense + roguelike

You defend your keep while actively fighting enemies in real time. Combat is automatic, but positioning, upgrades, relics, and timing decisions matter.

Short, fun rounds

Sessions are designed to be quick and rewarding. You can make meaningful progress in minutes, with depth for long runs and optimization.

Roguelike progression

Every run offers new choices: upgrades, cards, relics, and modifiers that change how the game plays. No two runs feel the same.

Mobile-first design

Clean UI, big buttons, readable numbers, and smooth animations. Everything is optimized for touch and portrait orientation.

Art style and visual direction

Stylized, colorful, cartoon-fantasy visuals that feel playful, readable, and modern.

Bright, saturated palette

Vivid blues for water, lush greens for land, bold UI colors for buttons and panels.

Soft, rounded forms

Chunky, friendly silhouettes with smooth shading and toy-like depth.

High contrast UI

Large, glossy buttons and icons that are readable at a glance.

Playful fantasy tone

Swords, relics, cards, coins, and gems presented in a light, approachable way.

Platforms

Hero King is launching on iOS and Android as a premium mobile experience with long-term progression, live updates, and ongoing content.
